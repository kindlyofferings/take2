Our Story

Born from a belief that shopping can be a small act of love — for people, for places, for the planet.

🌿

Who We Are

We started
with a question.

What if every purchase could feel good — not just in the moment, but in the long story of where it came from and where it ends up?

Kindly Offering began as a small curation project, gathering products that passed a simple test: is this genuinely better for the people who make it, use it, and eventually say goodbye to it?

We're a dropshipping store, which means we work directly with conscious suppliers around the world. No warehouses of excess inventory sitting unsold. No overproduction. Just the right things, sent directly to you.

How We Choose

Our standard.

Every product in our store passes at least three of these five tests before we carry it.

🌱

Natural Materials

Organic, recycled, or sustainably harvested wherever possible.

🏭

Ethical Production

Suppliers who pay fair wages and maintain safe working conditions.

♻️

End-of-Life Plan

Compostable, recyclable, or built to last a very long time.

📦

Minimal Packaging

No unnecessary plastic. No excessive boxes. Just the product.

💚

Genuine Purpose

Something that actually makes life better — not just greener-looking.

Shipping Policy

Everything you need to know about how we get your order to you.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 2–5 business days of payment confirmation. You will receive a shipment notification email with tracking information once your order has been dispatched.

Shipping Methods & Timeframes

We offer the following shipping options at checkout:

  • Standard Shipping — 7–14 business days (Free on orders over $60)
  • Express Shipping — 3–7 business days (additional fee applies)

As a dropshipping store, some orders may be fulfilled by different suppliers and may arrive in separate packages. You will receive individual tracking information for each shipment.

Free Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $60 USD. This discount is automatically applied at checkout.

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. International delivery times vary by destination and are typically 10–21 business days. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Carbon-Neutral Shipping

We are committed to reducing the environmental impact of delivery. Where available, we select carbon-neutral shipping options and offset emissions for shipments where these options are not yet available.

Lost or Delayed Packages

If your package has not arrived within the expected timeframe, please contact us with your order number and we will investigate promptly.

Address Accuracy

Please ensure your shipping address is correct at the time of ordering. We are unable to redirect packages once they have been dispatched and cannot accept responsibility for orders lost due to an incorrect address.

Return Policy

We want you to love what you receive. Here's how returns work.

Our Return Window

We accept returns within 30 days of the delivery date. Items must be unused, in their original condition, and in original packaging where applicable.

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please contact us with your order number and the reason for your return. We will provide you with return instructions and a return shipping label where eligible.

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or we made an error.
  • We recommend using a tracked shipping service for returns, as we cannot be responsible for items lost in transit.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you by email. Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days and credited to your original payment method. Please allow additional time for your bank to process the refund.

Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you would like a different item, please return the original product for a refund and place a new order.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Personal care items (soaps, shampoo bars, skincare) — for hygiene reasons
  • Items marked as final sale
  • Digital downloads or gift cards
  • Items that have been used, washed, or altered

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you.

Questions

If you have any questions about our return policy, please don't hesitate to get in touch.
