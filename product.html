This mat was born from a simple belief: that the things we press our bodies into every day should come from the earth, not harm it. Our Cork & Natural Rubber Mat is made in small batches from sustainably harvested cork oak bark — the tree is never cut down, only stripped every nine years — bonded to a base of natural tree rubber.

The result is a mat that breathes, grips, and ages beautifully. Cork is naturally antimicrobial, so it self-cleans between washes. The grip actually deepens when wet — perfect for hot yoga or a sweaty flow. And because it's free of PVC, synthetic foams, and toxic adhesives, you breathe only what the forest left behind.

Available in four earth tones. Each mat is made to order in our partner studio in Quito, Ecuador, and ships within 5 business days.
